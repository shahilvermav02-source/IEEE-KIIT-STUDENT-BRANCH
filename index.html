Achyuta Samanta

Achyuta Samanta is a visionary leader and philanthropist whose tireless work in education and social service has changed countless lives. Founder of KIIT and KISS, his dedication to providing education, healthcare and opportunity to underprivileged children demonstrates a rare blend of compassion and leadership. Under his guidance, institutions like KIIT have grown into world-class centres of learning while keeping human values at their core. His journey is an inspiration — a reminder that one person's selfless commitment can uplift whole communities.
